About Us


At Huncho Kickz our mission is simple: To see the joy in your face when your favorite pairs of shoes have been brought back to life.

From humble beginnings, Huncho Kickz was founded by Lloyd Alexander and Martyo Bethea in November of 2018 on the campus of North Carolina A&T. As two sneaker enthusiasts, these young men decided to offer a service that they felt was a necessity in the environment they were in. Huncho Kickz started as a sneaker cleaning/restoration entity solely operating off of Instagram, but has plans to offer our customers whatever service they are searching for, whether that be customizations or getting that brand new pair of sneakers you desire.
